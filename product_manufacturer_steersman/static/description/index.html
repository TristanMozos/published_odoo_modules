Product Manufacturers

Assign manufacturers and maintain MPN and MAP

This module will allow you to assign a manufacturer and maintain Manufacturers Part Numbers (MPN) and Minimum Advertised Pricing (MAP) for each product. In addition, it will make the MPN field searchable.

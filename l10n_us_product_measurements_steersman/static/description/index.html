Maintain product measurements in any UoM

Use metric or imperial units as most practical for your business

This module will allow you to maintain product weight, volume and dimensions in any UoM. Use metric or imperial units as most practical for your business!

Key Features

Product Dimensions

Our module adds length, width and height fields to products. The dimensions can be entered for both product templates and variants.

Use any UoM

Our module adds an option to select UoM when entering product weight, volume and dimensions giving you the flexibility to use metric or imperial units as most practical for your business.

Compatibility

The measurements entered are converted into default Odoo metric units and stored in the native fields, which means that our module preserves the native Odoo data structure and is fully compatible with any other modules that may use information from the native fields. In addition, this means that you will not lose the entered weight and volume information if you upgrade the database to a newer version of Odoo or decide to uninstall the module.
